# Course Crawler

![Screenshot 2024-01-22 022228](https://github.com/metechmohit/Course-Crawlers/assets/42698592/81864a8f-f1a4-4306-ae78-ad63cf368850)



Course Crawler is an automated software or web application designed to systematically and comprehensively search, collect, and present information about courses available on various online education platforms upon users' will. It offers a convenient solution for learners and educators by streamlining the process of discovering and managing courses.

## Specificity

![Screenshot 2024-01-22 022238](https://github.com/metechmohit/Course-Crawlers/assets/42698592/f7e706db-a7bb-460a-bc40-cb0046a50639)



## Features

- **Comprehensive Search:** Course Crawler conducts a thorough search across multiple online education platforms, ensuring users have access to a diverse range of courses.

- **Centralized Discovery:** Users can discover and explore courses from different platforms in one place, simplifying the process of finding relevant and high-quality educational content.

- **Filtering Options:** Course Crawler often provides advanced filtering options, allowing users to narrow down their search based on specific criteria such as subject, level, and duration.

- **User-Friendly Interface:** The interface is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, making it easy for learners and educators to navigate and find the information they need.

  ![Screenshot 2024-01-22 022249](https://github.com/metechmohit/Course-Crawlers/assets/42698592/3f893fca-a60c-45d8-8982-16e78efbb422)

## How to Use

1. **Search:** Enter your desired course or subject in the search bar.
2. **Filter:** Utilize the filtering options to narrow down your search based on specific criteria.
3. **Explore:** Browse through the results and click on a course to access detailed information.
4. **Platform Link:** When you find a course of interest, Course Crawler provides a direct link to the platform offering the course for enrollment.
